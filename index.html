rankly.

A simple tool to help you rank stuff

Please enter between 2 and 10 items.

Why only 10 items?

You're going to be shown every possible combination and asked to pick a winner. The formula for counting the number of pairwise combinations is:

n(n-1)/2

This means that 10 items produce 45 pair combinations. Any longer of a list would get pretty tiresome to rank!

Which is better?

If you see the same combination twice, it's because there's a tie and we're trying to find the winner!

Here's your ranked list!

    How is this calculated?

    Points are awarded each time an item is preferred. This final ranking shows all items in order from most points to least.
